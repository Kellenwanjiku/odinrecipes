Pilau

Description

Pilau is a popular dish in East Africa that is made with rice, meat, and spices. It is a flavorful and aromatic dish that is perfect for special occasions and celebrations. Pilau is often served with a side of salad or vegetables and is a favorite for many.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Wash the rice in cold water until the water runs clear. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and cook until softened.
  3. Add the cumin, coriander, turmeric, and cinnamon to the pot and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add the rice to the pot and stir to coat in the spices.
  5. Add 4 cups of water to the pot and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes until the rice is cooked and the water is absorbed.
  6. Fluff the rice with a fork and serve hot.
