Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish consisting of a round, flat dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings like meats, vegetables, and herbs, baked to perfection. It is known for its versatility, allowing endless combinations of ingredients to suit different tastes and preferences.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup pepperoni slices
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced onions
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness and shape.
  3. Transfer the dough to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  4. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a border for the crust.
  5. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Arrange the pepperoni slices, black olives, mushrooms, bell peppers, and onions on top of the cheese.
  7. Sprinkle the Parmesan cheese, fresh basil leaves, garlic powder, oregano, and red pepper flakes over the toppings.
  8. Drizzle the olive oil over the pizza.
  9. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  10. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool slightly before slicing and serving.
